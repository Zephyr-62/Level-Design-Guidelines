## Notation
Throughout the text you can find several words highlighted with both italics and bold notation. Here is a guide on how to interpret these words.

- Italics Uncapitalized: These words are used for soft emphasis in a paragraph.
- Bold Uncapitalized: These words are used for strong emphasis in a paragraph.
- Italics and Capitalized: These words highlight key concepts that can be found in the glossary e.g. *Vistas*, *One-way Valves*, or *Denial Spaces*
- Bold and Capitalized: These words highlight guidelines e.g. **Choices**, **POIs**, or **Spatial Communication**
- Games: Italics, Capitalized, and with the release date in parenthesis e.g. *Celeste (2018)* or *Sekiro (2019)*
