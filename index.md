## Notation
Throughout the text you can find several words highlighted with both italics and bold notation. Here is a guide on how to interpret these words.

- **Soft Emphasis**: Italics Uncapitalized e.g. "Some of the words are more *important* than others."
- **Strong Emphasis**: Bold Uncapitalized e.g. "The concept I explain in this **sentence** is crucial."
- **Glossary Key Concepts**: Italics Capitalized e.g. *Vistas*, *One-way Valves*, or *Denial Spaces*
- **Guidelines**: Bold Capitalized e.g. **Choices**, **POIs**, or **Spatial Communication**
- **Games**: Italics Capitalized, and release date in parenthesis e.g. *Celeste (2018)* or *Sekiro (2019)*
